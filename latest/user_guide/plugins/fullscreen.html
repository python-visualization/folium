Fullscreen#

Add a button to toggle a fullscreen view of the map.

[2]:

m = folium.Map(location=[41.9, -97.3], zoom_start=4)

folium.plugins.Fullscreen(
    position="topright",
    title="Expand me",
    title_cancel="Exit me",
    force_separate_button=True,
).add_to(m)

m
